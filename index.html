ISM High Performance Computing Conference, Tokyo, Japan - Oct 9-10

The International Conference on High Pefromance computing will take plac at the Institute of Statistical Mathematics, Tokyo Japan, 9-10 August 2015.

The conference aims at bringing together researchers and practitioners to discuss recent developments in computational methods, methodologies for big data analysis and applications in cloud computing. Topics include, but are not limited to: simulation distributed computing big graph cloud computing in Education virtuaization bio informatics
