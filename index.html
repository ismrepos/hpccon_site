ISM High Performance Computing Conference, Tokyo, Japan - Oct 9-10

The International Conference on High Performance computing will take place at the Institute of Statistical Mathematics, Tokyo Japan, 9-10 October 2015.

The conference aims at bringing together researchers and practitioners to discuss recent developments in computational methods, methodologies for big data analysis and applications in cloud computing. Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Simulation
  • Distributed computing
  • Big graph
  • Big Data in clouds
  • Cloud computing in Education
  • Virtualization
  • Bioinformatics
